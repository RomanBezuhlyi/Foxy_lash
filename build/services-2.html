Introducing Hydrafacial treatments at Foxy Lash Boutique!

Hydrafacial is a globally recognized skincare brand for its innovative facial, body and scalp rejuvenation and nourishing treatments. With its patented technology and personalized approach, Hydrafacial has become a trusted name in the beauty industry.

what is HydraFacial Treatment?s

HydraFacial treatment is a non-invasive, multi-step facial rejuvenation procedure that combines cleansing, exfoliation, extraction, hydration, and antioxidant protection. It uses a unique vortex suction technology to deeply cleanse and hydrate the skin, leaving it refreshed, revitalized, and glowing.

Clients undergoing HydraFacial treatment have the option to personalize their experience by selecting from various boosters tailored to address specific skin concerns. Choices include collagen for skin elasticity, energizing for revitalization, or clarifying boosters for congested skins.

benefits for your skin

HydraFacial offers a multitude of benefits for the skin, including:

  • Deep Cleansing

    Removes impurities, dirt, and oil from pores for a clearer complexion.

  • exfoliation

    Gently removes dead skin cells, promoting smoother and softer skin texture.

  • Hydration

    Infuses the skin with moisture, leaving it hydrated.

  • extraction

    Clears out debris and blackheads from pores, reducing congestion.

  • antioxidant protection

    Delivers antioxidants to the skin, helping to combat free radical damage.

  • Non-invasive

    Gentle and comfortable procedure with no downtime, suitable for all skin types.

  • Instant results

    Immediate improvement in skin tone, texture, and radiance after just one session.

  • Long-term benefits

    Regular treatments can help maintain skin health, improve elasticity, and reduce the signs of aging.

HydraFacial Scalp Treatment

Innovative procedure designed to rejuvenate and nourish the scalp, promoting healthier hair growth and overall scalp health. Using the same patented vortex suction technology as the traditional HydraFacial, this treatment deeply cleanses the scalp, removes buildup, and unclogs hair follicles. It also provides hydration and delivers essential nutrients to the scalp, such as peptides, vitamins, growth factors and antioxidants, which can help strengthen hair follicles and improve hair quality.

The treatment is soothing and comfortable, with no downtime, making it suitable for all hair types and scalp conditions. It's an effective way to address issues like dryness, flakiness, excess oil production, and even mild scalp conditions like dandruff.

The HydraFacial Scalp Treatment is typically recommended as a three-month program, with treatments scheduled one month apart for optimal results. Consistency is key to achieving amazing results, as each session builds upon the previous one to improve scalp health, promote hair growth, and enhance overall hair quality.
