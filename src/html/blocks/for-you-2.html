HydraFacial Scalp Treatment

Innovative procedure designed to rejuvenate and nourish the scalp, promoting healthier hair growth and overall scalp health. Using the same patented vortex suction technology as the traditional HydraFacial, this treatment deeply cleanses the scalp, removes buildup, and unclogs hair follicles. It also provides hydration and delivers essential nutrients to the scalp, such as peptides, vitamins, growth factors and antioxidants, which can help strengthen hair follicles and improve hair quality.

The treatment is soothing and comfortable, with no downtime, making it suitable for all hair types and scalp conditions. It's an effective way to address issues like dryness, flakiness, excess oil production, and even mild scalp conditions like dandruff.

The HydraFacial Scalp Treatment is typically recommended as a three-month program, with treatments scheduled one month apart for optimal results. Consistency is key to achieving amazing results, as each session builds upon the previous one to improve scalp health, promote hair growth, and enhance overall hair quality.
