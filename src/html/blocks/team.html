Meet Inna

“Hi, my name is Inna! My passion for beauty started at 14 years old. I loved to make women look more beautiful, than they already were. I was 19 years old when I moved from Ukraine to New York. I worked a few jobs out of the beauty industry until I finally made the decision in 2012 to go to beauty school, so I could get my license and start working with women. I started doing makeup and became very successful, working on brides and famous people but I wanted more. That's when I decided to start doing lashes and permanent makeup. I started doing lashes and PMU in 2016 and moved to LA, so I had to start from zero. I built a large clientele and was finally happy doing what I always wanted to do since I was 14 years old. In July of 2023 I decided to move to Miami. Now here I am! Starting from zero again but very excited for what's coming. I hope to see you at Foxy soon.”

Meet Bahar

“Hi! I am Bahar. It´s my nickname that everyone calls me! I was born in 1990 and grew up in Iran. I started permanent makeup when I was 18 years old and shortly after learned how to do lash extensions. I love doing these services because they stimulate my love for art and beauty! I moved to Turkey when I was 24 years old. It was challenging being far from my family, learning a new language, meeting new people and finding work; but I did it and became one of the best in the city! Today I’m here in South Florida with new challenges and starting once again. So far it has been great! I have been with Foxy Lash Boutique for over a year now and love it more and more every day! The girls here are real artists full of compassion, inspiration and good energy. I love pampering my clients and love making them feel more beautiful than they already are. Hope to see you soon!”
