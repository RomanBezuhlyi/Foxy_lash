Permanent makeup

Permanent makeup involves applying pigments to the skin's dermal layer to enhance features like eyebrows, eyeliner and lips. It's a long-lasting solution for a makeup-like appearance.

  • Nano Brows

    Nano brows are a cutting-edge technique in brow permanent makeup. Using a fine, nano-sized needle, pigments are meticulously implanted into the skin to create ultra-realistic hair strokes. This technique allows for precise control and natural-looking results, mimicking the appearance of real eyebrow hairs.

  • Powder Brows

    This popular method involves depositing pigments into the skin using a shading technique to create a soft, powdered effect. The result is a fuller, more defined brow shape that resembles the look of filled-in eyebrows with makeup. Powder brows are a great option for those who prefer a more polished and filled-in appearance.

  • Microblading

    Popular technique in eyebrow permanent makeup that creates natural-looking, fuller eyebrows. It involves using a handheld tool with tiny, U-shaped blades to manually deposit pigments into the skin, creating hair-like strokes that mimic the appearance of real eyebrow hairs.

    $500

  • Brow Lamination

    Brow Lamination is a great way to get a temporary brow lift/ wispy brows. It brings the hairs up and back to life, making any brows full and beautiful. It fills in gaps in the brows and gives the illusion of fullness and growth in a fast, noninvasive way. You can add a brow cleanup or a brow tint to this service!

    $100

  • Brow Henna

    Henna is a form of temporary staining that can last up to 5 weeks on hair AND up to 2 weeks on skin, depending on your skin type. It gives a makeup/powder brow look without any makeup at all.

    $40

EYES

This technique involves the application of pigments along the lash line to create a long-lasting and defined eyeliner look. It is a form of cosmetic tattooing that can eliminate the need for daily eyeliner application. The color and thickness of the eyeliner can be tailored to suit individual preferences and desired outcomes. The results can last for several years, gradually fading over time.
